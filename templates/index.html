Visit Serengeti!

In the Serengeti, lions can be seen roaming the grassy plains, resting under the shade of acacia trees, or hunting for prey. They are formidable predators and primarily feed on large herbivores such as wildebeests, zebras, and gazelles, which are abundant in the Serengeti.
